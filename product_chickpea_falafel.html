Chickpea Falafel

Authentic Middle Eastern falafel made from chickpeas, onions, garlic and spices. Crispy on the outside and tender inside, perfect as a snack or main dish.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Herbs (parsley, coriander)
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, pepper)
  • Salt

Nutritional Values (per 100g)

Energy720 kJ / 170 kcal
Fat8 g (saturated 1 g)
Carbohydrates15 g (sugars 2 g)
Protein8 g
Salt1.0 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Deep fry: Fry at 180°C for 3–4 minutes.
  • Oven: Bake at 200°C for 10–12 minutes.
  • Pan: Cook with oil for 6–8 minutes.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at -18°C. Shelf life 18 months.

Packaging

400 g pouch – retail; 2 kg box – food service.
