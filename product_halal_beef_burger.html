Halal Beef Burger

Juicy beef burgers made from certified halal beef. Ideal for grilling, these burgers are seasoned to perfection for a rich, satisfying flavour.

Ingredients

  • Halal beef (98%)
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Salt

Nutritional Values (per 100g)

Energy1000 kJ / 240 kcal
Fat17 g (saturated 7 g)
Carbohydrates5 g (sugars 0.5 g)
Protein18 g
Salt1.3 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Grill: Cook on medium-high heat for 12–14 minutes, turning once.
  • Pan: Cook in oil for 8–10 minutes.
  • Oven: Bake at 180°C for 15–18 minutes.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at -18°C. Shelf life 12 months.

Packaging

500 g pack – retail; 2.5 kg pack – food service.
