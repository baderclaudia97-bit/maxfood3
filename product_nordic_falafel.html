Nordic Falafel

This Nordic‑inspired falafel contains 82 % yellow peas grown in Sweden. Soft on the inside and lightly crisp on the outside, they pair well with salads, wraps or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • Yellow peas (82%)
  • Onion
  • Parsley and dill
  • Rapeseed oil
  • Salt, white pepper, cumin

Nutritional Values (per 100 g)

Energy820 kJ / 196 kcal
Fat9 g (saturated 1 g)
Carbohydrates19 g (sugars 2 g)
Protein8 g
Salt1.0 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Oven: Bake at 200 °C for 10–12 minutes until golden.
  • Pan: Heat a little oil and fry for 6–8 minutes, turning often.
  • Air fryer: 200 °C for 8–10 minutes.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at –18 °C. Shelf life 18 months. Once thawed, use within 24 hours.

Packaging

400 g bag appropriate for retail.
