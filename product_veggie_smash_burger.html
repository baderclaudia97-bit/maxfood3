Veggie Smash Burger

Our Veggie Smash Burgers are thinner patties designed to be cooked hot and fast, giving you a crispy edge with a tender plant‑based interior. Each 360 g pack is perfect for quick weeknight meals.

Ingredients

  • Plant‑based protein (soy and pea)
  • Vegetable oils (sunflower, coconut)
  • Onion powder
  • Natural flavours
  • Salt and pepper

Nutritional Values (per 100 g)

Energy800 kJ / 190 kcal
Fat10 g (saturated 2 g)
Carbohydrates5 g (sugars 0.5 g)
Protein19 g
Salt1.4 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Pan/Skillet: Preheat lightly oiled pan and cook from frozen for 3 minutes per side.
  • Griddle: Smash onto hot griddle and cook for 2–3 minutes per side.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at –18 °C. Shelf life 12 months.

Packaging

360 g retail pack.
