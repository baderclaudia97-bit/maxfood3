Swedish Meatballs

Classic Swedish meatballs made with a blend of beef and pork, onions and spices. Perfect for both retail and food service customers looking for authentic taste and convenience.

Ingredients

  • Beef (60%)
  • Pork (30%)
  • Onion
  • Breadcrumbs (wheat)
  • Eggs
  • Spices and salt

Nutritional Values (per 100g)

Energy900 kJ / 215 kcal
Fat13 g (saturated 5 g)
Carbohydrates10 g (sugars 1 g)
Protein14 g
Salt1.2 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Oven: Preheat oven to 180°C. Bake for 15 minutes.
  • Pan: Heat oil in pan. Cook for 8–10 minutes, turning occasionally.
  • Air fryer: Cook at 200°C for 12 minutes.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at -18°C. Shelf life 12 months. Do not refreeze once thawed.

Packaging

500 g bag – retail; 2 kg bag – food service.
