Traditional Falafel

Our Traditional Falafel is available in generous 800 g and 3 kg bags for restaurants and catering. Hand‑crafted flavour with a crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (80%)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Herbs (parsley, cilantro)
  • Sunflower oil
  • Salt, cumin, coriander

Nutritional Values (per 100 g)

Energy790 kJ / 189 kcal
Fat8 g (saturated 1 g)
Carbohydrates19 g (sugars 2 g)
Protein7 g
Salt1.2 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Deep fry: 175 °C for 3–4 minutes until golden.
  • Oven: Bake at 200 °C for 15 minutes, turning once.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at –18 °C. Shelf life 12 months.

Packaging

Available in 800 g and 3 kg packs for food service.
