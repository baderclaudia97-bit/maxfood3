Healthy Falafel

Our Healthy Falafel is free from gluten, lactose and soy, making it a great option for a wide range of diets. Each 600 g bag contains bite‑sized falafel balls made from wholesome chickpeas, herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (78%)
  • Onion & garlic
  • Parsley and coriander
  • Sunflower oil
  • Salt, cumin and pepper

Nutritional Values (per 100 g)

Energy750 kJ / 180 kcal
Fat8 g (saturated 1 g)
Carbohydrates18 g (sugars 2 g)
Protein6 g
Salt1.1 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Oven: Bake at 200 °C for 12–15 minutes until crisp.
  • Pan: Fry in a little oil over medium heat for 5–7 minutes.
  • Air fryer: 200 °C for 10 minutes.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at –18 °C. Shelf life 18 months. Once thawed, consume within 24 hours.

Packaging

600 g resealable bag suitable for retail.
