Vegan Burger

A delicious plant-based burger crafted from a blend of peas, beans and wheat protein. Our vegan burgers deliver a hearty bite and savoury flavour, ideal for vegans and meat‑lovers alike.

Ingredients

  • Pea protein
  • Bean and soy protein
  • Wheat protein
  • Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Spices and salt

Nutritional Values (per 100g)

Energy800 kJ / 190 kcal
Fat9 g (saturated 2 g)
Carbohydrates7 g (sugars 1 g)
Protein18 g
Salt1.5 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Oven: Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes.
  • Pan: Cook with a little oil for 6–8 minutes per side.
  • Grill: Grill on medium heat for 10 minutes.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at -18°C. Shelf life 12 months. Do not refreeze after thawing.

Packaging

400 g pack – retail; 1.8 kg pack – food service.
