Swedish Classic Meatballs

A true taste of Sweden, these classic meatballs are ready in minutes. The 400 g pack is perfect for quick dinners, combining juicy meat with a savoury gravy suggestion printed on the back.

Ingredients

  • Pork and beef (80%)
  • Onions
  • Bread crumbs (wheat)
  • Egg powder
  • Potato starch
  • Salt and spices

Nutritional Values (per 100 g)

Energy880 kJ / 210 kcal
Fat14 g (saturated 5 g)
Carbohydrates8 g (sugars 1 g)
Protein15 g
Salt1.3 g

Cooking Instructions

  • Oven: Heat at 200 °C for 15 minutes from frozen.
  • Pan: Brown over medium heat for 8 minutes.
  • Microwave: 750 W for 4 minutes, stir halfway.

Storage & Shelf Life

Keep frozen at –18 °C. Shelf life 12 months.

Packaging

400 g retail bag.
